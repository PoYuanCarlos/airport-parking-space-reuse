# Scenario Assumptions

## Scenario Levels

| Scenario | Shared Autonomous Mobility Adoption | Interpretation |
|---|---:|---|
| Low | 10% | Limited market adoption; parking demand remains dominant. |
| Medium | 25% | Noticeable access-mode shift; curbside and staging pressure increase. |
| High | 45% | Strong parking demand reallocation; reuse strategy becomes important. |
| Disruptive | 65% | Aggressive long-term planning scenario; high uncertainty. |

These assumptions are illustrative and should be labeled as synthetic unless supported by a specific public source.
